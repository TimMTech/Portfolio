03 : About me

My Name Is Tim

I am a full stack developer from New York City. I enjoy taking complex computer problems and turning them into simple and easy-to-use, user-friendly interfaces. I also love the logic and structure of coding and always strive to write and execute efficient code, whether it be Python, Javascript, HTML, CSS etc. Although THE most important quality I possess is the hunger to always learn new material.

Nothing brings me more pleasure than making something out of nothing. Even when the results are far from my ideal expectations. The entire ceremony is surreal to me. The only disadvantage that I have possess is the constant notion of "How can I make this even more extravagant." I am never fully satisfied. Maybe that is an advantage, who knows. Only you can be the judge of that.

As a Person

I like to think of myself as an energetic, happy person. In most of my free time, I enjoy traveling, going to the gym, watching comedy movies and working on coding projects that may potentially be useful to the population. I like to think of myself as a hero. Always lending a helping hand.
